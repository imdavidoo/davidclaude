# Exercise Collection

Exercises for micro-habit sessions at home — during work, breaks, meals, etc.

## Rings — Strength
- Pull-ups
- Chin-ups
- Diagonal leg raises

## Bodyweight — Strength
- Pistol squats *(practicing — ankle flexion limits range, heel lifts off ground)*

## Cardio at Home
- Jump rope
- High jumps (tuck jumps / squat jumps)
- Mountain climbers
- Burpees / military burpees
