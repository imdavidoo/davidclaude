# Argentina Packing List — Feb/Mar 2026

**Constraint**: Carry-on only. KLM international + JetSmart/Aerolíneas Argentinas internal flights.

**Climate**: Late summer. Buenos Aires hot (25–30°C). Patagonia variable (5–20°C, strong wind, possible rain). Bariloche moderate (10–22°C).

## Hiking & Outdoor
- Hiking boots (worn on flights to save bag space)
- Windbreaker (doubles as rain jacket)
- 1 pair quick-dry hiking pants
- 2–3 moisture-wicking shirts/base layers
- Warm fleece or mid-layer
- Hiking socks × 3
- Daypack (bought in Buenos Aires)
- Sunglasses (bought in Calafate — lost original on airplane)
- Sunscreen (high SPF — Patagonia sun is strong)
- Hat/cap
- Buff/neck gaiter (wind protection in Patagonia)
- Warm beanie (Patagonia cold mornings + windy ridges)
- Thin gloves
- Thermal base layer (long-sleeve)
- Trekking poles: renting locally at each stop

## City / Casual
- 2–3 t-shirts
- 1 pair shorts (Buenos Aires heat)
- 1 pair casual pants or jeans
- Sneakers or casual shoes
- Running shoes + running gear
- Flip flops / sandals

## Essentials
- Passport
- Phone + charger + charging cables (USB-C)
- Reusable water bottle (1L+ for hike days)
- Headlamp (early starts on hikes)
- Basic first aid: ibuprofen, anti-diarrhea, blister plasters
- Fluticasone nasal spray
- Toiletries (travel-size), beard cream, sunscreen (face), comb, deodorant
- Pen (customs/immigration forms)
- Small padlock (hostel lockers)
- Insect repellent / DEET (dengue active in BA)

## Nice to Have
- Earplugs + eye mask
- Ziplock bags (rain protection for phone/docs on hikes)
- Portable power bank
- Whoop charger
- Kindle
- Blue light glasses
- Swimwear (Bariloche lakes)
- Neck pillow
- Headphones

## NOT Packing (rent/buy locally)
- Trekking poles — rent at each hiking destination
- Heavy winter gear — not needed in late summer
